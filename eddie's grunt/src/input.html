Things have been really busy here at Litmus over the past couple of months. We've hired another developer, Jonathan, and launched a bunch of new things. Read on for details.

Email, Twitter and IM notifications

Wouldn't it be nice to be alerted when your email or browser test is complete? Now you can set up notifications so you'll receive an email, a Twitter message or an IM. It means you can get on with something else whilst you're waiting for your test results.

Thunderbird and Mobile Me added

We've added Thunderbird (versions 2 and 3) and Mobile Me to our email client line up. These are available for use immediately, inside your Litmus account. Also, here's a sneak peek at what's coming soon.

Automate your Litmus testing

Some of our customers have built some very clever systems to help them run Litmus tests more easily. Here's how Yahoo uses Litmus as part of their deployment process, and how LUON built an application to automate their email tests.

Updated browsers

We've updated Internet Explorer, Safari, Firefox, Opera and Google Chrome to their latest versions. All these are available now inside your account.

Campaign Monitor partnership

Campaign Monitor is an email marketing tool aimed specifically at web designers. We've partnered with them to give their users access to Litmus. You can purchase individual email tests from right inside Campaign Monitor.
